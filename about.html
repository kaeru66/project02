About Us

- Since 1990 -
SHUEN FUH, a professional and reliable hardware and giftware manufacturer, specialize in R&D on Press Mold and Injection Mold since 1990. The current product items we produce are retractable badge reels, card holders, retractable key chains, badges, safety pins, baby pins, ID badge clips, customized lanyards, decorative nails, dressmaker pins and key chain accessories included phone straps, ball chains, and swivel clips. Shuen Fuh's engineers are experienced and professional, any of the items that relate to press mold and injection mold are welcome. Shuen Fuh is 100% accepted the OEM &ODM order and customized enquiries.
Shuen Fuh set up the rule to produce the quality products with 1%~5% defected rates. The products are well necessary to go through the double check before making the shipment to client’s hands. The products go through the inspection first after that it goes to the next process on package. Shuen Fuh products are successfully and widely spread to the Europe countries, the Middle East Countries, Australia and Japan. Our main target is to use Shuen Fuh’s profession and service to work with each countries of the world.

Any of the items we can produce or able to produce, it is our pleasure to hearing from your enquiries. We will quote the best and competitive price for your reference. Any comments and suggestions for Shuen Fuh are highly appreciated.
